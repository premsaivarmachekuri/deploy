IEEE Computer Society Gitam Chapter

IEEE Computer Society Gitam Chapter is a professional chapter of the IEEE Computer Society, which is is a growing and diverse community of computing professionals and the most trusted source for information, inspiration, and collaboration in computer science and engineering, focusing on the regions of the state of Andhra Pradesh, India
